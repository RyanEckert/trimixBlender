This tool is a mathematical model and is intended for planning purposes only.
Real-world variables (gauge accuracy, gas purity, and adiabatic heating) will affect results.
Real gas calculations assume a settled, constant temperature.
Filling generates heat; pressures must be adjusted or cylindera allowed to cool.

Handling pure oxygen is inherently dangerous.
Ensure all equipment is O₂ clean and follow proper partial pressure blending protocols.
You must always independently verify O₂ and He content with calibrated analyzers.
Never dive a gas you have not personally analyzed and signed for.
